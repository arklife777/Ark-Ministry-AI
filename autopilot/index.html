What it does

Four jobs, on a schedule, without you

The agent wakes itself on a timer, does the work, and drops finished posts into your queue days ahead — so there's always a window to glance before they go live.

Writes

Drafts captions in your church's voice — devotionals, event promos, verse-of-the-day, reminders.

Designs

Generates a matching image for every post, so nothing goes out as plain text. No Canva sessions.

Schedules

Queues everything into your channels days in advance — with a built-in window to approve or tweak.

Talk to it on Telegram

The agent isn't a black box — it's a conversation. Text it to create a post on the spot, approve what's queued, reschedule, or pause for a week. It texts you back when posts are ready to review.

"make a post about Sunday""show me what's queued""approve all""skip this week"
Near-autonomous, with a net. Posts schedule a few days out, not instantly — and Telegram pings you to review. Hands-off, not blind.
