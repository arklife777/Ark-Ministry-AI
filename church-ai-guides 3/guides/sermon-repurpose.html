Guide 03

One Sermon into Ten Pieces

Take Sunday's message and break it into clips, a devotional, a blog post, and study questions — a week of content from one sermon.

▶
Video walkthrough coming here
Drop in a YouTube/Vimeo embed or screen recording.
Step 1

Pick your passage and audience

Decide the scripture and who it's for — youth group, adult small group, new believers. This shapes the tone of everything the AI produces.

Step 2

Use the starter prompt

Paste the provided prompt, swap in your passage, and let the AI draft an outline, discussion questions, and leader notes.

Step 3

Refine and personalize

Ask for simpler language, more questions, or a specific theological angle. Two or three follow-ups gets it just right.

Step 4

Format and hand it out

Turn the result into a clean one-pager you can print or share. We'll show you the fastest way to do this.
